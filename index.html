Assignment 2

1. Parrot
This program checks your input and answers accordingly.
2. Your 1 Gs Anniversary Calculator
This program asks you about your birthday and gives you the date on which will be 1 Giga seconds old.
3. Leap Year Calculator
This program takes a year as an input from you and tells you whether this year is leap or not.
4. Historical Adventure Game
This text-based game is based on imagining that you stumble upon a time machine that can take you any year you want and ask how you react in each senario.
5. Is this your number?!
This is a game in which you are to choose a number between 1 and 31, shows you some tables and tells you your secret number at the end!
